# Product

## Register

product

## Users
One person: the operator who runs automated Roblox bots across multiple games. Power user, technical, wants dense information at a glance. Context: monitoring hundreds of accounts, checking stats, performing bulk operations.

## Product Purpose
Internal dashboard for managing and monitoring hundreds of Roblox game accounts. Tracks account stats (gold, gems, prestige, spins, inventory, perks), online status, and provides bulk operations. Success = instant situational awareness across all accounts.

## Brand Personality
Efficient, dense, precise. No marketing fluff. The UI should feel like a command center, not a consumer app. Three words: **ruthless, fast, sharp**.

## References
- Linear: keyboard-first, dense, dark-native, surgical UI
- Raycast: speed-oriented, minimal chrome, instant feedback

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS landing pages
- Rounded pastel cards with big whitespace
- Gradient text or glassmorphism
- Consumer onboarding flows
- Marketing hero sections
- Colorful illustration-heavy dashboards

## Design Principles
- Information density over whitespace
- Scan-ability: numbers should pop, status should be instant
- Keyboard-first where possible
- No decoration that doesn't serve comprehension
- Speed is a feature: every interaction should feel instant

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG AA minimum: contrast ratios, keyboard navigation, focus indicators
- Reduced motion respected via prefers-reduced-motion
- No reliance on color alone for status (pair with icons/text)
